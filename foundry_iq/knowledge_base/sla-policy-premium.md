# AMOMS-X SLA Policy — Premium Tier
Policy ID: SLA-PRE-001
Version: 3.0

## Applicability
Customers with annual contract value between $50,000 and $500,000 USD.

## Delivery Commitments
- Standard orders: 3 business days
- Express orders: 1 business day
- Same-day orders: by 2:00 PM local time

## SLA Breach Thresholds
- Warning: ETA exceeds required date by more than 2 hours
- Breach: ETA exceeds required date by more than 8 hours
- Critical: ETA exceeds required date by more than 24 hours

## Escalation Procedure
1. Warning: automated update plus notify account manager
2. Breach: account manager contacts customer within 1 hour. Carrier escalation initiated within 2 hours.
3. Critical: VP Operations notified immediately. Alternative carrier activated.

## Critical Rule for High Value Orders
For orders exceeding $10,000 USD carrier escalation must be initiated within 2 hours of ETA breach detection regardless of breach severity level.

## Compensation
- Breach: 20% discount on current order shipping
- Critical: full order refund option
