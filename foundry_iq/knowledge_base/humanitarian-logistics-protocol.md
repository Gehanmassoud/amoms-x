# Humanitarian Logistics Protocol
Document ID: OPS-HUM-001

## Purpose
NERVA applies to humanitarian operations including food banks, disaster relief, and refugee supply chains. In these contexts a missed delivery is not an inconvenience. It is a crisis.

## Priority Classification
- Tier 1: Medical supplies and medications — immediate escalation
- Tier 2: Food and water for vulnerable populations
- Tier 3: Shelter and clothing supplies
- Tier 4: General humanitarian aid

## Vulnerable Population Protection
Beneficiary data including names, locations, and vulnerability classifications must be protected at all times. Purview sensitivity labels apply to all beneficiary records. Agents cannot expose individual beneficiary details in outputs.

## Immune Agent Humanitarian Rules
When distribution route disruption detected:
1. Immediately assess impact on vulnerable populations
2. Prioritize rerouting for Tier 1 and Tier 2 supplies
3. Apply vulnerability weighting to beneficiary prioritization
4. Notify field coordinators within 15 minutes
5. Log all decisions with full audit trail for donor reporting

## Donor Reporting
All autonomous decisions must be logged for donor transparency. Purview lineage tracking provides complete audit trail from data source to delivery confirmation.

## Same System — Higher Stakes
The same NERVA agent mesh that detects a carrier failure affecting commercial orders detects a distribution disruption affecting families before they miss their weekly food delivery. Same architecture. Same agents. Infinitely higher stakes.
