# AMOMS-X SLA Policy — Enterprise Tier
Policy ID: SLA-ENT-001
Version: 2.0

## Applicability
Customers with annual recurring revenue exceeding $500,000 USD.

## Delivery Commitments
- All orders: 99.5% SLA guarantee within committed window
- Emergency orders: 2-hour delivery guarantee in select markets
- Dedicated carrier for orders exceeding $50,000

## SLA Breach Thresholds
- Warning: ANY deviation from committed window
- Breach: ETA exceeds committed window by more than 1 hour
- Critical: ETA exceeds committed window by more than 4 hours

## Escalation Procedure
1. Warning: immediate notification to dedicated account team
2. Breach: COO notified. Dedicated carrier activated. Customer executive contact within 30 minutes.
3. Critical: crisis response protocol activated. Full executive escalation chain engaged.

## Compensation
- Any breach: service credit equal to 100% of order value
- Critical breach: full refund plus dedicated service review
