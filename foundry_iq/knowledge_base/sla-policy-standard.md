# AMOMS-X SLA Policy — Standard Tier
Policy ID: SLA-STD-001
Version: 1.0

## Applicability
Customers with annual contract value below $50,000 USD.

## Delivery Commitments
- Standard orders: 5 business days
- Express orders: 2 business days
- Same-day orders: by 6:00 PM local time

## SLA Breach Thresholds
- Warning: ETA exceeds required date by more than 4 hours
- Breach: ETA exceeds required date by more than 24 hours
- Critical: ETA exceeds required date by more than 48 hours

## Escalation Procedure
1. Warning: automated customer notification
2. Breach: assign to logistics coordinator within 4 hours
3. Critical: escalate to Operations Manager within 2 hours

## Compensation
- Breach: 10% discount on next order
- Critical: full refund of shipping costs
